Canning: A Complete Guide

Canning is a method of preserving food in airtight containers to extend its shelf life.

Types of Canning

  • Water Bath Canning: Used for high-acid foods like fruits and pickles.
  • Pressure Canning: Used for low-acid foods like meats and vegetables.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Prepare jars by sterilizing them.
  2. Fill jars with food, leaving headspace.
  3. Seal with lids and rings.
  4. Process jars using a water bath or pressure canner.
  5. Cool and store in a dark place.
